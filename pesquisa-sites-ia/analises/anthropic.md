## 3. Color Palette

Anthropic uses a distinctive warm, organic palette that deliberately distances itself from typical "tech blue" aesthetics:

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Primary Dark | Off-Black | `#141413` |
| Primary Light / Background | Warm Off-White | `#FAF9F5` |
| Mid Gray | Muted Stone | `#B0AEA5` |
| Light Gray | Warm Light | `#E8E6DC` |
| Accent Orange | Terracotta | `#D97757` |
| Accent Blue | Muted Sky Blue | `#6A9BCC` |
| Accent Green | Sage Green | `#788C5D` |
| Brand Tan | Warm Brown | `#D4A27F` |
| True Black | Black | `#191919` |
| True White | White | `#FFFFFF` |

**Design Philosophy:** "Warm, trustworthy, and thoughtfully restrained. Anthropic's identity pairs an organic tan palette with clean typography to communicate safety-focused AI research — approachable yet serious, human yet technical."

The color system was specifically developed to bring warmth to the brand while handling the dual needs of marketing communications and product UI.

## 4. Typography

| Usage | Font Family | Foundry |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| Headlines & Subheadings | **Styrene** | Commercial Type (Berton Hasebe) |
| Body Text | **Tiempos** | Klim Type Foundry |
| UI/System fallback | Poppins (headings 24pt+), Lora (body) | Google Fonts fallbacks |
| Fallback stack | Arial, Georgia | System |

**Typography Character:** The Styrene family is described as "technically refined and charmingly quirky" — it has geometric forms with subtle humanist details. Tiempos is a contemporary serif that provides warmth and readability in body copy.

The pairing of a geometric sans (Styrene) with a refined serif (Tiempos) creates a tone that is simultaneously technical and approachable — a deliberate design choice reflecting Anthropic's dual identity as a research lab and consumer product company.

---

## 5. Visual Effects

- **No heavy animations or particle effects** — The design is intentionally restrained
- **Smooth scroll transitions** between sections
- **Subtle fade-in animations** on content as it enters viewport
- **No grain, no blur effects, no WebGL, no 3D** — stands in stark contrast to typical AI company aesthetics
- **Clean, flat design** with generous whitespace
- **Warm photography** with natural tones (not dark/neon tech aesthetic)
- The visual approach is deliberately anti-futuristic — designed to feel human and trustworthy rather than intimidating

---

## 6. Image/Video Style

- **Real photography** — candid, warm, human-centered images
- **Natural tones** — earth tones, warm lighting, avoiding cold/blue tech aesthetics
- **Product screenshots** shown clean, without excessive styling
- **Featured stories** use editorial-quality photography (e.g., NASA/Mars imagery for the Perseverance story)
- **No abstract AI art**, no neural network visualizations, no robot imagery
- **No neon glows or dark-mode-only presentation**

## 8. Tone of Voice

- **Formal but warm** — Academic rigor meets accessible communication
- **Safety-first messaging** — Every product description connects back to responsible development
- **Understated confidence** — Avoids hype, focuses on capability descriptions
- **Research-oriented** — Uses language from academic and policy worlds
- **Human-centered** — Talks about what people can accomplish, not about the AI's power

---

## 9. How They Talk About AI Without Cliches

Anthropic carefully avoids:
- "Revolutionary" / "game-changing" / "cutting-edge"
- Robot imagery or sci-fi references
- Neon/futuristic visual metaphors
- "AI will change everything" proclamations

**Instead they use:**
- "Reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems"
- "AI for Problem Solvers" — positions AI as a tool, not a sentient being
- "Tackle complex challenges" — action-oriented, practical language
- "Genuinely helpful conversations" — emphasizes utility over novelty
- "Safety-focused AI research" — leads with responsibility
- "Think through your hardest work" — collaborative framing

**Key language patterns:**
- AI as a partner/tool, never as a replacement
- Focus on what the human accomplishes, with AI as enabler
- Safety and responsibility integrated naturally, not as disclaimers
- Technical capability described through practical outcomes

## Key Takeaways for FlowAI Digital

1. **Warm color palette breaks AI conventions** — The tan/terracotta palette is a masterclass in differentiation. FlowAI should note how warmth builds trust without sacrificing professionalism.
2. **Typography pairing creates dual identity** — Geometric sans + refined serif = technical + human. This is exactly the balance FlowAI needs for dental/aesthetic clinic audiences.
3. **Restraint is powerful** — No animations, no flashy effects, no dark mode. The design says "we're serious about our work" through what it doesn't do.
4. **Ad-free positioning** — Anthropic explicitly calls out what they don't do (ads, sponsored content). FlowAI could similarly position by calling out what they're NOT (not a marketing agency).
5. **Safety as brand identity** — Anthropic turned a constraint (AI safety concerns) into a differentiator. FlowAI can do the same with their "engineering, not marketing" positioning.
