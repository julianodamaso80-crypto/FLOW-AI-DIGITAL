# Cohere.com — Site Analysis

> **Research date:** April 2026
> **URL:** https://cohere.com
> **Category:** Enterprise AI Platform / NLP

---

## 1. SEO Metadata

- **Title tag:** "Enterprise AI: Private, Secure, Customizable | Cohere"
- **Meta description:** Cohere builds powerful models and AI solutions enabling enterprises to automate processes, empower employees, and turn fragmented data into actionable insights.
- **North product page title:** "North: The AI Platform Where Work Flows | Cohere"
- **Positioning keywords:** Enterprise AI, private AI, secure AI, customizable, NLP, language models

---

## 2. Hero Headline

**Current homepage headline:** Enterprise AI positioning focused on privacy, security, and customization.

**North platform tagline:** "From scattered tools to seamless action — North brings everything together so your work just works."

**Supporting copy:** "Build automations, chat with your data, and co-create with AI from a secure, intelligent workspace."

The language is practical and enterprise-focused. "Private, Secure, Customizable" in the title tag are three concrete, verifiable promises — not abstract vision.

### Extended "New Nature" Palette (Pentagram)
| Color Category | Color Name | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Natural tone | Coniferous Green | Deep forest green — nature, stability |
| Natural tone | Mushroom Grey | Warm neutral — organic, grounded |
| Natural tone | Volcanic Black | Deep rich black — depth, authority |
| Synthetic tone | Simulated Coral | Vibrant coral/orange — energy, warmth |
| Synthetic tone | Synthetic Quartz | Crystal-like pink — innovation, precision |
| Synthetic tone | Acrylic Blue | Bright artificial blue — computing, technology |

### Design Approach
- **"New Nature" concept:** Color palette merges natural tones (greens, greys, blacks) with synthetic/computing hues (coral, quartz, blue)
- **Gradient atmospheres:** Extended color combinations create textured backgrounds without dominating layouts
- **The coral accent (#FF7759) prevents the brand from feeling clinical** — adds human warmth
- **Vibrant gradients:** Used as a distinctive visual feature, data-rich dashboard aesthetic

## 5. Typography

### Custom Type System (by Pentagram + NaN foundry)
| Typeface | Weights | Style | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Cohere Text** | Bold, Regular, Light + Italics | Faux-mono that references digital, code-centric world | Body copy, UI elements |
| **Cohere Outline** | Display weight | Characters with Voronoi cell cuts auto-dispersed through characters | Headlines, marketing |
| **Cohere Mono** | Monospace | Code-optimized | Code blocks, technical docs |

### Key Typography Details
- **Cohere Text** uses a "faux mono" style — proportional spacing but the aesthetic of monospace, bridging design and code
- **Cohere Outline** is unique: the letterforms have Voronoi pattern cuts that automatically appear as you type — the brand literally lives inside the typography
- The headline typeface was built specifically for "Cohere For AI" (research division)
- All three typefaces created by NaN foundry in collaboration with Pentagram

---

## 6. Visual Effects

- **Voronoi patterns:** The core visual motif — tessellations found in giraffe coats, dragonfly wings, and mathematical diagrams. Appears across all brand touchpoints.
- **3D cell animations:** Dynamic Voronoi cells that connect to form an abstract "C" letter
- **Gradient atmospheres:** Smooth color transitions create depth without heavy visual elements
- **Subtle animations:** Cells expand and contract, suggesting organic growth
- **Custom Cinema 4D tool:** Bespoke 3D tool created for generating Voronoi-based brand visuals
- **Bespoke Figma plugin:** Internal design tools for consistent pattern application
- **No grain, no particles, no WebGL on the homepage:** Effects are implemented through CSS gradients and SVG patterns
- **Clean data visualization aesthetic:** Dashboard-influenced layout with clear information hierarchy

---

## 7. Image / Video Style

- **Voronoi pattern graphics:** Primary visual element — organic cells in brand colors
- **3D rendered cells:** Cinema 4D-generated visuals of the Voronoi language
- **Product interface screenshots:** Clean shots of North platform, API playground
- **Abstract "new nature" imagery:** Visuals that blend organic and synthetic aesthetics
- **Monolinear icons:** Each icon = single continuous line within 24px square, inspired by Voronoi language
- **No stock photography:** Brand relies entirely on custom visual system
- **No people photos on homepage:** Focus on the abstract visual identity and product UI

## 9. Tone of Voice

- **Register:** Enterprise-professional, measured, trust-building
- **Approach:** Security-first communication — leads with privacy and control, then capability
- **Vocabulary:** "Private," "secure," "customizable," "deploy," "automate," "empower"
- **Key pattern:** [Security assurance] + [capability statement]
- **What they avoid:** No consumer-friendly simplifications. Speaks to CTO/CISO concerns directly.

---

## 10. How They Talk About AI Without Cliches

Cohere's language is enterprise-precise:

- **"Enterprise AI: Private, Secure, Customizable"** — Three adjectives that are auditable, not aspirational. A security team can verify each claim.
- **"Turn fragmented data into actionable insights"** — Acknowledges a real enterprise pain (fragmented data), offers a concrete outcome (actionable insights)
- **"From scattered tools to seamless action"** — Problem-solution structure without hyperbole
- **"Your work just works"** — Intentionally simple. Echoes Apple's "it just works" philosophy.
- **"New Nature"** brand concept — Frames AI as organic evolution, not disruptive revolution
- **No "transform your business," no "unlock potential," no sci-fi language**
- **"Sovereign AI"** — Addresses specific enterprise concern (data sovereignty) rather than making capability claims
- **Voronoi pattern = visual metaphor:** The organic pattern says "AI is natural evolution" without writing it in copy

---

## 11. Key Design Insights for FlowAI

1. **Custom typography IS the brand:** Cohere Outline with Voronoi cuts makes the type system unique to Cohere
2. **Nature metaphors for AI build trust:** "New Nature" makes AI feel evolutionary, not threatening
3. **Security-first messaging for enterprise:** Leading with "Private, Secure" addresses the #1 enterprise AI concern
4. **Warm accent breaks clinical feel:** #FF7759 coral adds approachability to an otherwise serious palette
5. **Three-typeface system creates hierarchy:** Text (body), Outline (headlines), Mono (code) — each has clear purpose
6. **Voronoi as brand DNA:** One core visual concept, applied everywhere, creates instant recognition
7. **Custom tools for brand consistency:** Figma plugins and Cinema 4D tools show the investment in visual system
