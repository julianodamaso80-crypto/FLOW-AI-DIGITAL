## 2. Hero Headline

**Current headline:** Focused on "The most realistic voice AI platform" / all-in-one creative platform messaging.

The hero communicates breadth of capability (speech, video, music, sound effects) while anchoring on their core strength: voice realism. The word "realistic" does the heavy lifting — it's a provable claim, not subjective marketing.

## 4. Color Palette

### Primary Colors (from Brand Guidelines & Brandfetch)
| Color Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Black | #000000 | 0, 0, 0 | Primary background, logo default |
| White | #FFFFFF | 255, 255, 255 | Primary canvas, text on dark |
| Dove Gray | #6E6E6E | 110, 110, 110 | Secondary text, muted UI |
| Concrete | #F2F2F2 | 242, 242, 242 | Light surfaces, cards |

### Extended Color System
- **9 distinct hues** with **11 tints each** (99 total accent colors)
- Vibrant tints used for marketing assets
- Represents diversity of human expression
- Primary palette stays monochrome; color is reserved for moments of impact

### Design Approach
- **Monochrome foundation:** Black and white dominate, reflecting engineering precision
- **Color as event:** Vibrant hues appear only in specific marketing contexts
- **High contrast:** Stark black/white ensures visibility from app icons to large displays
- Black = precision, technology, professionalism
- White = clarity, accessibility, clean canvas

## 5. Typography

### Font System
- **Wordmark:** Custom typeface — subtly condensed with squarish shapes
- **Body/UI:** Clean sans-serif (geometric, optimized for screen)
- **Special typefaces:**
  - **Chime** — Used for captioning; synchronizes written text with generated speech
  - **Echo** — Blends Sound Flow layers with typography; expresses depth and emotional range

### Hierarchy
- Large, bold headlines
- Moderate body text for readability
- Monospace or technical type for code/API sections

---

## 6. Visual Effects

- **Chladni patterns:** Variations of Chladni patterns serve as primary graphic elements — visual representations of sound waves and vibration nodes
- **Kinetic typography:** Text that moves and responds, reflecting the audio-first nature of the product
- **Audio waveform visualizations:** Real-time canvas-based waveforms using requestAnimationFrame for 60fps rendering
- **Bar visualizer components:** Audio-reactive bar graphs
- **Live waveform scrolling:** Historical average audio volume displayed as scrolling bars (timeline view)
- **Shimmering text effects:** Custom text animation components
- **Matrix-style visual components:** Data/text grid animations
- **Smooth state transitions:** Between active, processing, and idle states
- **Dark cinematic UI:** Audio-waveform aesthetics throughout
- **No 3D / WebGL / particles:** Effects are 2D and tied to audio visualization

---

## 7. Image / Video Style

- **Audio waveform visualizations:** Primary visual motif — sound made visible
- **Product screenshots:** Clean UI of the voice generation interface
- **Demo audio players:** Embedded players showing voice quality
- **Abstract sound patterns:** Chladni patterns create organic, scientific-looking visuals
- **No stock photos of people talking:** They avoid the cliche of "person with headset"
- **Minimal illustration:** Design system is functional, not decorative

## 9. Tone of Voice

- **Register:** Technical-yet-accessible, confident, creator-focused
- **Approach:** Practical and direct — tells you what you can make, not how impressed you should be
- **Vocabulary:** "Create," "generate," "clone," "design," "compose," "deploy"
- **Key pattern:** Action verbs + creative outcomes
- **Avoids:** No "magic," no "amazing," no hyperbole about quality — lets demos speak

---

## 10. How They Talk About AI Without Cliches

ElevenLabs' language is distinctly practical:

- **"Ultra-realistic speech"** — "Realistic" is a measurable quality claim, not vague praise
- **"The most powerful AI audio APIs"** — "Powerful" is generic but anchored to "APIs" (developer-specific, not consumer fluff)
- **"Create controllable, expressive speech"** — "Controllable" and "expressive" are specific, functional descriptors
- **"Studio-quality"** — Benchmarks against real-world standard (recording studios), not against other AI
- **"All-in-one platform"** — Positions as practical tool consolidation, not futuristic vision
- **No robot imagery, no brain graphics, no "sentient AI" language**
- **Voice diversity framing:** They frame their tech through human expression diversity, not AI capability

---

## 11. Key Design Insights for FlowAI

1. **Monochrome as premium signal:** Black + white creates perceived value and seriousness
2. **Sound visualization as brand identity:** Chladni patterns and waveforms make invisible tech visible
3. **Color as strategic accent:** Using color sparingly makes moments of color more impactful
4. **Custom typography as differentiator:** Chime and Echo typefaces create unique brand recognition
5. **60fps micro-interactions:** Waveform animations are technically impressive but purposeful
6. **"Start Creating for Free"** — CTA that communicates both action and zero-risk entry
